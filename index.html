ROYAL ENFIELD GT 650

The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is a Neo-retro Cafe Racer motorcycle produced by Royal Enfield (India). The first model to use the name, the Continental GT 250, was produced by the original Royal Enfield in the 1960s UK.The name was revived by the Indian manufacturer in the 2010s with the Continental GT 535 (now discontinued) and Continental GT 650.

GT NEW LOOK

The cafe racer has either a standard dual-seat or an optional solo seat a seat hump.The fuel tank is more angular than the roadster's.Clip-on handlebars and rear-set pegs allow a sportier riding.The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is powered by a 647.95 cc,parallel-twin, air cooled and oil cooled engine.
